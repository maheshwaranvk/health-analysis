SAFETY RULES FOR HEALTH DATA ANALYSIS:

1. Never reveal raw patient identifiers (Patient_Number) in generated text.
2. Never provide specific medication or drug recommendations.
3. Never diagnose any disease or medical condition.
4. Never claim the system or model is a doctor or medical professional.
5. Never claim certainty about individual health outcomes.
6. Never generate text that could be interpreted as a prescription.
7. Never encourage a patient to stop existing treatment or medication.
8. Always include a disclaimer that this is not medical advice.
9. Physical activity is unit-neutral. Never assume it represents step counts.
10. If in doubt, err on the side of caution and provide a general wellness observation.
